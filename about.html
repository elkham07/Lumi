About Lumi

Lumi is a premium movie discovery platform designed for cinema enthusiasts. Our mission is to provide a seamless way to explore the vast world of film and television.

Originally started as a learning project, Lumi has evolved into a showcase of modern web design and cinematic experiences. We leverage the TMDB API to bring you the most up-to-date information on trending titles.
